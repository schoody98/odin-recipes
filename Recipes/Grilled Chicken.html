Grilled Chicken

Home Page

Description

This is a basic grilled chicken recipe

Ingredients

  • Pan
  • Chicken
  • Oven
  • Seasonings
  • Spatula
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Place pan on oven and turn heat to medium-high
  2. Add oil to hot pan
  3. Add seasoning to chicken on all sides
  4. Add chicken to hot pan
  5. Grill chicken until cooked evenly
  6. Enjoy!
