Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a basic macaroni and cheese recipe that anyone with some simple cooking equipment can do!

Ingredients

  • Oven/flat top surface burner
  • Stainless steel pot
  • Noodles
  • Velveeta
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Shredded cheese
  • Wooden spoon
  • Noodle drainer

Steps

  1. Add water to pot
  2. Boil water
  3. Add noodles to boiling water and let cook for approximately 10 minutes or until noodles are soft
  4. Drain water from noodles
  5. Add noodles back to pot
  6. Add milk and cheese
  7. Stir until cheese is melted
  8. Sprikle shredded cheese over top of the noodles
  9. Serve and enjoy!
