Iced Coffee

Home Page

Description

This recipe is for a basic iced coffee drink. This coffee can be made with a standard coffee maker or a Keurig.

Ingredients

  • Coffee maker
  • Coffee
  • Water
  • Creamer
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Fill coffee maker/Keurig with water
  2. Brew your favorite coffee
  3. Let coffee stand/cool down
  4. Add ice to coffee
  5. Add creamer to taste preference
  6. Enjoy!
